Kalaripayattu is a traditional martial art from Kerala focused on flexibility, strength, and body control.

Training Approach

Our curriculum honors ancient traditions through modern, rigorous conditioning methods:

  • Intensive body conditioning
  • Traditional leg techniques
  • Advanced flexibility training
  • Foundational weapon training

Progression

Student advancement is measured through mastery of physical and mental capabilities:

Body Strength Total Flexibility Absolute Control

Testing & Evaluation

Regular performance evaluations ensure students have mastered the fluid yet powerful movements required of the art.

Demonstrations & Showcases

Participate in elite demonstrations and cultural showcases to celebrate the heritage and skill of Kalaripayattu.

The Benefits

Kalari training provides superior flexibility, explosive strength, mental discipline, and a deep sense of cultural awareness.
