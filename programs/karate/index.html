Karate is a structured martial art based on striking techniques such as punches, kicks, and blocks, combined with disciplined movement and controlled application.

Training Approach

  • Basic techniques (Kihon)
  • Forms (Kata)
  • Sparring (Kumite)
  • Step-by-step movement mastery

Progression

White Yellow Orange Green Blue Brown Black

Testing & Evaluation

Official 3–6 month belt tests include technique evaluation and formal certification from STADO International.

Competitive Exposure

Students gain exposure through District, State, and National championships alongside regular Inter-Dojo events.
